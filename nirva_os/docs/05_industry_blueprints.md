# 05 — Industry Blueprint Library

Source: [`../data/blueprints.json`](../data/blueprints.json). Each blueprint is a
**sellable starter-kit**: structure, risks, required standards, core processes,
mandatory documents, audits, training, reports and dashboards — so a new
customer in that industry is productive on day one instead of month six.

Blueprints are also a **revenue stream** in their own right (productized
consulting — see [09_revenue_gtm.md](09_revenue_gtm.md)).

## Anatomy of a blueprint (example: Cleaning Service)
- **Structure:** site supervisors → cleaning crews → QC inspectors → contract managers
- **Top risks:** ghost/no-show staff · quality complaints · chemical safety · headcount SLA breach
- **Standards:** ISO 9001, 45001, 14001, 10002, PDPA
- **Process:** roster → geo check-in → checklist completion → QC inspection → complaint handling → billing reconciliation
- **Documents:** service SOPs, site work instructions, chemical SDS, QC checklists, contract & SLA
- **Audits:** site QC audit · headcount reconciliation · safety inspection
- **Training:** method SOP · chemical handling · PPE/safety · customer conduct
- **Reports:** attendance-vs-required · QC pass rate · complaint resolution · SLA compliance
- **Dashboards:** live staffing per site · QC trend · complaint heatmap

## Why this wins
For BEST's three core lines (cleaning, FM, security), NIRVA ships a **complete,
audit-ready operating model** — not a blank database. The same blueprint sold to
the next cleaning company is near-zero marginal cost. As Future Ventures adds
Manufacturing, the Machine Shop / Industrial-ERP blueprints are already drafted.
