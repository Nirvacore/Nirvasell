# 00 — Source of Truth

## What NIRVA is
| NIRVA is NOT | NIRVA IS |
|---|---|
| ERP | **Workforce & Operations Operating System** |
| HR / Payroll software | **Operational Integrity Platform** |
| A time-clock app | **Organizational Intelligence Platform** |
| An AI chatbot | **Knowledge Transfer Platform** |
| A Thai accounting system | **Compliance Platform** |

NIRVA is the layer an organization *runs on* — not another app it has to
integrate. It is to a service business what an operating system is to a
computer: the thing everything else sits on.

## The pilot: BEST SERVICE
- Cleaning · Facility Management · Security · Investigation · Rental · Manpower
- 1,356+ employees, 256+ sites
- Real pains: LINE check-in (no integrity), Excel sprawl, PDF sprawl, scattered
  data, knowledge trapped in people, painful audits, blind executives.

BEST is NIRVA's *sandbox of truth* — every feature is proven on a real,
labour-intensive, multi-site operation before it is sold.

## Philosophy — the line we never cross
NIRVA exists to:
- increase **transparency**, **trust**, **fairness**
- reduce **conflict**, **rework**, **loss**
- protect the **dignity** of workers
- transfer **knowledge** and help people **grow**
- let the organization improve **without depending on any one person**

NIRVA does **not** exist to catch people, spy on people, control people, or run
on fear. *Verification builds trust; surveillance destroys it.* Every design
decision is tested against this line — e.g. check-in proves "I was here and
safe," it is not a tracking leash.
